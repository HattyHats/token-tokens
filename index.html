How To Read A Crypto Chart

From complete beginner to professional — everything you need to understand what price is doing and why it matters.

🕯 Candlestick Anatomy
🟢 Bullish (UP)
🔴 Bearish (DOWN)

Each candle = one time period


Body = Open vs Close range

Wicks = extreme prices reached

Green = price went UP that period

Red = price went DOWN that period

📈
Trend
Uptrend — Higher highs and higher lows. Each peak and valley is above the last.

Downtrend — Lower highs and lower lows.

Sideways — Price bouncing between two levels. Wait for a breakout before trading.
🏗
Support & Resistance
Support — A floor where buyers historically step in. Price bounces upward from here.

Resistance — A ceiling where sellers appear. Price struggles to break above.

Once broken, resistance often becomes new support.
📦
Volume
Volume = how much was traded that period.

High volume on up move = strong buying.
High volume on down move = strong selling.
Low volume move = weak, likely to reverse.

Always confirm price action with volume!
〰️
Moving Average (MA / EMA)
Smooths price over a set period to reveal the true trend direction.

Price above MA = bullish. Below MA = bearish.
Golden Cross (fast MA crosses above slow MA) = major bullish signal.
EMA reacts faster than MA to recent moves.
🌡
RSI — Momentum Meter
Relative Strength Index (0–100) measures how fast price is moving.

Above 70 = Overbought — may pull back soon.
Below 30 = Oversold — may bounce soon.

RSI divergence: price makes new high but RSI doesn't = warning signal!
⚡
MACD — Trend Momentum
Shows momentum shifts using two moving averages.

MACD crosses above signal = bullish momentum.
MACD crosses below signal = bearish momentum.

Watch the histogram — when it shrinks, the trend may be weakening.
📐
Bollinger Bands
Middle MA with upper/lower bands based on volatility.

Near upper band = potentially overbought.
Near lower band = potentially oversold.
Band squeeze = volatility about to expand — big move incoming!
💧
VWAP
Volume Weighted Average Price — used by professional/institutional traders.

Price above VWAP = bullish intraday sentiment.
Price below VWAP = bearish intraday sentiment.

Acts as a key dynamic support/resistance level throughout the session.
⚡ Live Analysis Checklist
  • What is the overall trend? (Higher highs = uptrend, lower lows = downtrend)
  • Is price above or below MA(20) and EMA(50)? (Above = bullish, below = bearish)
  • Is volume confirming the move? (High volume = stronger signal)
  • Where are key support and resistance levels? Is price testing one now?
  • Is RSI above 70 (overbought) or below 30 (oversold)? Any RSI divergence?
  • Is MACD crossing up (bullish) or down (bearish)?
  • Are Bollinger Bands squeezing? (Big move likely coming)
  • Is price above or below VWAP? (Institutional sentiment guide)
  • Any key candlestick patterns? (Long wicks = rejection, Doji = indecision)
  • Zoom out to the weekly chart — what is the bigger picture telling you?
